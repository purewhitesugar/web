Meet our talented staff here at Headhunters!

Theresa Baggett

Stylist/Colorist

Theresa has been with Headhunter's for 19 years and has been a joy to work with. She is a master stylist with an advanced background in color techniques such as highlighting and color repair.

Sandi Kelley

Colorist/Stylist

Three years and still shining at Headhunter Hair Styling. As she continues to grow her business she is ever expanding on showing her knowledge of the beauty industry through coloring, cutting, styling and even waxing. She enjoys creating fabulous up-do's for any formal event.

Paula Finley

Stylist/Colorist

A Master Stylist since 1980. Paula relocated from California in 2006 as an educator and platform artist. She excels in wedding styles. She can do Make-up, formal Up-do's along with creative color and cutting techniques.
